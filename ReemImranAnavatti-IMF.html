Problem Statement

To build a PowerBI dashboard to analyze the GDP growth.

Data Source

The IMF website that brings together data for the major economies available from international agencies covering the financial, governmental, external, and real sectors, and provides links to data in web sites of international and national agencies is the source of data.

Result

A dashboard that shows the GDP growth of each country and analyzes the comparison between the countries.
